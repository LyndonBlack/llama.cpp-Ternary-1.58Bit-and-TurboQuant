How Bubble Sort Works

Bubble Sort repeatedly steps through the array, compares adjacent elements, and swaps them if they are in the wrong order. Each pass pushes the largest unsorted value to its correct position (like a bubble rising). The process repeats until no more swaps are needed.

Time Complexity: O(n²) comparisons  |  Space Complexity: O(1) auxiliary
